AIPPM

AIPPM is a meeting that allows the diverse political groups of India to reach a consensus on decisions of national importance before the Parliament sessions begin. The purpose of the forum is to provide better insights into national issues. This committee aims to delve into various matters, discussing solutions and representing the diversity in thought and community in our country.
