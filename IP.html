IP

IP serves as the media arm of an MUN, reporting on the activities and developments throughout the event. Journalists representing various press agencies actively observe and document the proceedings, capturing moments, conducting interviews, and gathering information from various committees.

Background Guides

A Background Guide is an introductory document curated by the Executive Board. It briefs about matters related to the committee and its agenda. You can find all the Background Guides by using the button below.
