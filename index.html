About Us

At Delhi Public School Hyderabad, we recognize the incredible potential of students to engage in meaningful debate and diplomacy. To this end, we are committed to providing a platform where young minds can hone their skills and articulate solutions for pressing global issues.
We are excited to announce DPSHMUN 2024, a Model United Nations conference to be held on August 22nd and 23rd. We aim to offer students a unique opportunity to engage in structured debates, assume the roles of delegates from various nations, and explore the intricacies of international relations and diplomacy.

The Delhi Public School Hyderabad cordially invites participation from its student fraternity to discuss and debate the pressing issues of our time.

Join us for this exceptional event, fostering diplomacy, critical thinking, and global awareness.

Let us Debate Discuss & Deliberate!

Letter from the Secretary-General

Dear Delegates,

It gives me immense pleasure to host you at the 2024 edition of Delhi Public School Hyderabad Model United Nations conference. As this edition of the DPSHMUN commences, it continues to serve as a platform for inquisitive minds to engage in constructive dialogue and collaborative problem-solving.

At its essence, a Model United Nations conference is a means through which students improve their dexterity in the areas of diplomacy, debate, and conflict resolution. In light of the increasingly precarious state of global issues, it becomes imperative for young individuals to foster such skills and shoulder the burden of addressing pressing matters. Nurturing such problem-solvers is at the core of our conference’s existence. We aim to simulate circumstances that mirror pivotal decision-making environments in order to instil an ethos of altruism and consciousness within our participants, all in line with our guiding principle of ‘Service before Self’.

On behalf of the Secretariat, I would like to express my gratitude to all the participants for their enthusiasm. We remain steadfast in our commitment to fostering an inclusive learning environment for all. At this year’s edition of DPSHMUN, we bring to you a wide range of committees, from ones that tackle issues related to international peace and security to others that deal with matters related to sporting events. We promise to leave you with a new perspective on pertinent issues thereby inspiring you to take meaningful action.

We eagerly look forward to welcoming you on the 22nd and 23rd of August at the Senior Secondary Campus of Delhi Public School, Hyderabad.

Regards, Yamini Tumu Secretary-General, DPSHMUN 2024
