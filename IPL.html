IPL

IPL oversees India's premier Twenty20 cricket league. It manages ten teams representing different cities, ensuring a blend of sportsmanship and economic viability. IPL tackles various challenges including security, economic fluctuations, and ethical considerations, while innovating to maintain the league's integrity. As a pivotal entity in Indian cricket, IPL shapes the sport's future amidst a global landscape.
